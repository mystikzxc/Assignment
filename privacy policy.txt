Privacy Policy for Course Registration - Mystik

Introduction

Welcome to Course Registration - Mystik, a demo Android application that allows users to register for courses. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information provided by users of our application.

Since we do not collect any user information, we have no data to use, share, or sell to third parties. Our primary goal is to offer a seamless course registration experience without compromising your privacy.

Security

We take security seriously, even though we don't collect user data. Our application is designed to function securely without the need for personal information storage. We regularly update and maintain our app to ensure the best possible security.

Cookies

Course Registration - Mystik does not use cookies or any tracking technologies to collect information about its users.
